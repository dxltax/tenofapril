Because most days,
I still think you’re the one I miss the most.
The one whose brief warmth
gave me strength through my longest nights.
Like the early bloom of spring,
you made me believe
I could start again.

🍂

Most days,
I still wait for you,
as if seasons could turn backward,
as if the trees would bear leaves again
just because I kept standing still.

🍂

And even now,
after all the silence,
after being left like the last leaf
clinging to a bare branch…

🍂

I know this,
If you returned
on another autumn wind,
I would still open the door.

🍂

Even if I knew
you’d bring the fall with you again.
Even if loving you
meant losing myself once more.

🍂

That’s how deeply I loved you.
And how hopelessly
I still do.
