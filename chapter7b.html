What do I do
with a body that remembers your touch
better than it remembers its own worth?

🍂

I feel like a tree stripped bare too soon…
still standing,
but no longer proud.

🍂

This skin,
this face,
this frame of me,
still carries the warmth of your old words,
but it’s fading,
like golden leaves
turning brittle in the wind.

🍂

I am trapped in an autumn
you left behind in me.

🍂

And I don’t know
how to bloom again
without the sunlight of your eyes.
