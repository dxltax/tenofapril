I won’t chase you
through the forest of what-ifs anymore.
But I still love you—
like the last golden leaf
clinging to a branch,
not to be owned,
but to be remembered
in silence.

🍂

And yes…
in my quiet selfishness,
I still hope the trees whisper my name
when the wind brushes your skin.

🍂

Not to bring me back,
but to remind you
that once,
you were loved by something
that didn’t ask to last forever.

🍂

If the red string between us
was never meant to tie,
then let us become
two leaves from the same autumn—
blown apart,
but once trembling
on the same breath of wind.
