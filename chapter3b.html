Now I only have your old messages,
the withered petals of what once bloomed.
I reread them in silence,
each word a fallen leaf I press between pages of memory.
They hurt, yes,
but they also remind me,
once,
I was held by a warmth that felt like spring
before it turned to fall.

🍂

Even if your heart no longer carries that season for me,
believe this,
I will wait.

🍂

Through endless dusk,
through leafless trees and cold mornings,
I will wait.

🍂

Because after you,
no one feels like sunlight anymore.
It’s as if every color in me
has fallen with you.
