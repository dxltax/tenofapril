I : If Reincarnation Were Real

Even if the universe gave me a thousand lives,
your name would still be the loudest prayer I whisper.
I would choose you, again and again,
even if fate writes the same or a different ending.

To love you,
is a choice I’ve never once regretted,
not even when the past left me aching.

And if I were given just one more chance
to find you in another lifetime,
I wouldn’t just love you like I did yesterday—
I would love you deeper, stronger,
until time itself forgets how to measure love.

I wouldn’t care
if our story ends in joy or crumbles in silence.
This feeling is one I never wish to bury.

Because you,
are the one soul most worthy of my love
with all of my heart,
until I run out of seconds and destinies to give.

Xo, Delta
