I remember how I bloomed in your warmth,
like trees set aflame in amber and crimson.
And I remember too,
how everything began to change
when the wind grew colder.

🍂

Suddenly,
your warmth became rare,
your colors faded,
and all the things that once made me feel held
began to fall away.

🍂

I remember how the silence crept in
like the first frost,
how I begged you to stay
even as your footsteps disappeared
into a season I wasn’t ready for.

🍂

I dropped my pride like brittle leaves,
scattered it at your feet,
hoping you'd return for even one last look.

🍂

But the trees don't beg for their leaves.
They stand bare
and learn how to survive the cold.
