Everything about you once made my world brighter.
Your presence lit up the early hours of autumn,
a warmth that whispered,
“Stay a little longer.”

🍂

And I fell.
Slowly, deeply.
With every kind gesture,
with every reply you sent between your busy hours.
You made me feel seen,
held,
important.

🍂

But I also remember the silence that followed.
How your warmth faded,
like the last golden leaves drifting from the trees.
I began to suffocate in the emptiness you left behind.
Like the air had thinned.
Like nothing could bloom again.

🍂

And some mornings,
I wished I wouldn’t wake up,
because each sunrise only reminded me
that you were no longer here
to color the world like you used to.
