When the Leaves Forgot to Stay

I remember it all,
the way your gaze made the color rush to my cheeks,
like early autumn sunlight warming trees before they begin to let go.
I remember how your smile pulled my world out of orbit,
and how your voice made even silence feel full.

I remember how we spoke until 2 AM,
and by 4, I already missed you.
Like the last leaf still clinging to the branch,
I held on tightly,
even as the wind warned me
you were about to fall away.

Your name was a season in my chest.
Every message from you was a flicker of golden light
dancing across the forest of my day.
Even in chaos, I rushed to your words,
like a child chasing leaves down a quiet path.
You made everything feel alive.
