The Warmth That Faded with the Leaves

I still remember,
how your gaze made me blush like the first hint of sun after a long cold.
How your smile felt like sunlight slipping through yellowing leaves.
How your voice…
gentle and steady,
felt like the wind that rustles softly through a quiet afternoon.

I remember how we talked until 2 AM,
and how by 4 AM.,
I was already longing for you again.
Like trees aching for leaves they’d just begun to lose.

I still remember,
the sound of your messages,
how they rang differently from everyone else’s.
How I’d open them even in the middle of the busiest days,
like pausing under a tree just to watch the light fall through the branches.
