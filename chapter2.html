When Autumn Came Too Soon

h1>

I still remember
how your eyes caught mine,
shy and sudden,
like sunlight slipping through half-turned leaves.
How your smile made everything else blur.
How your voice
felt like warm wind brushing through early blossoms,
a promise that spring would stay.

I remember our midnight calls,
how your words lingered like golden hour light,
soft and slow and hard to forget.
By 4 AM,
I already missed you.
Your name on my screen
was the only thing louder
than the quiet in my chest.
