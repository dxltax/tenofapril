You used to trace me with longing
in hushed, forbidden ways,
exchanging secrets beneath the surface
like leaves pressed between pages
we never meant to close.

🍂

Now, my body feels like a forest in late
November…
bare, silent,
still waiting for your hands
like trees waiting for a spring
they know will not return.

🍂

Even the fog outside seems to know your name.
It lingers.
Just like you.

🍂

The universe feels like it’s conspiring
to keep you etched into every falling thing,
every amber leaf that spins to the ground,
every chill that steals warmth from my breath.

🍂

So tell me,
how do I find peace
when even the season won’t let me forget you?

🍂

I am the tree you left behind,
and everything in me
is still reaching for the wind
that once called me beautiful.
