Stay…
as the last warmth
beneath this fading sky.

🍂

Stay…
as the echo in the trees
before they stand bare.

🍂

Stay…
as the golden leaf that clings,
even as its brothers fall.

🍂

Because if you vanish now,
if you drift like the rest,
where will I go,
when even the trees have forgotten how to hold me?

🍂

You were never mine to keep.
But if you must go,
at least wait
until the last color fades,
until I am ready
to stand in the cold
without you.
