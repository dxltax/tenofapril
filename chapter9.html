Stay Until the Last Color Fades

I knew.
I always knew,
that one day,
the season would take you from me.

I knew the wind would change,
 that our paths would scatter like leaves beneath it.
 I knew
 our red thread was frayed from the start,
 never truly tied,
 just wrapped loosely
 around something fleeting.
What bloomed between us
 was always meant to wither.

Still,
wasn’t staying a choice
we could have made?

Couldn’t we have walked through the fall together,
just a little longer,
before the last leaf lets go?
