Ten of April — The Beginning of the End

If I hadn’t replied to your DM that day, maybe I wouldn’t have fallen this deep...

But…
If I had never met you, maybe I would’ve never known what it feels like to be truly worthy of love.

After my last heartbreak, I was completely broken... Until I met you…

How could that happen?
How did you do it?
Was I too easy to fall for you?
Or were you just too perfect to ignore?

Yes, you were that perfect...

I still remember clearly how our random conversations made me never want to stop talking to you...

Even though, in the end, the universe said otherwise.

April 10th, 2025
This is where my suffering began.
