The Season You Brought

I still remember it clearly,
how God brought you into my life in the simplest way.
A brief introduction, a few lighthearted conversations,
and somehow… you slowly became part of my days.
Something began to grow between us,
like tiny buds at the end of bare branches,
as if we were two seasons quietly meeting.

Ironically, I was in one of my most fragile phases back then,
heartbroken, still aching from wounds that hadn’t healed.
And yet, your presence felt like a warm breeze slipping through the chill.
Soothing.
For a while, I even forgot I was ever in pain.
